Animazement: Raleigh NC!!

Post 05/05/16

Well the summer is almost here folks, and for attendees at this years animazement, things with be back in swing starting May 27th-29th. So whip out that old cosplay that has been collecting dust in your closet,(I know you want to!!) or put together something quick for the time is near. If you haven't ever attended a comic or anime convention in the past and you are interested in going to one, i can say from experience that animazement is a lot of fun. Things kick off with the thursday night pre-con the 26th, with this years theme being "SPACE". Voice actress and pop-singer Aya Hirano will be joining as a special guest at the con, and there will be a special symphony saturday night conducted by japanese composer Toshiyuki Watanabe and the Duke University String School!. How cool is that. If you haven't got your ticket yet or are thinking about attending you can still purchase tickets at the door starting at thursday's pre-con. See you there!

ERASED is an anime you won't want to miss

Post 04/21/16

Boku Dake ga Inai Machi (僕だけがいない街?, lit. The Town Where Only I am Missing) is a psycological thriller anime that incorporates time travel, mystery, and suspense in a way that will leave you on the edge of your seat. Man, i tell you, the cliffhangers in this show are brutal leaving the episode ending just a something very vital and life threatning is happening to the characters. Speaking of characters, the ones in this show are good! In the very first episode you are almost instantly connected emotionally to the protraganist Satoru Fujinuma 藤沼 悟 and his troubling past. Poor hinazuki....... no spoilers .... i promise... you will find out exactly what i mean just a few episodes in to the show. Airi Katagiri 片桐 愛梨 is just a doll of a girl that works at the pizza shop with Satoru who has a very positive outlook on life and encourages Satoru to believe in himself. Seriously this girl is strong, emotionally and physically (you'll see just what i mean as you watch the show; lets just say she packs a punch!!) If you haven't gotten a chance to view this show, please do...... you will not.... i repeat... you will not be dissappointed. click here for the chrunchyroll simulcast.

DD シノン Sinon Gets Locked & Loaded!

Post 04/07/16

Volks has done it once again and uleashed yet another limited Dollfie Dream into the pool. Sinon from the popular anime series Sword Art Online was released as a part of the Dolls Party 33 Event in Akihabara. Fans Will know that she is not a gal to mess with as she comes packaged with her signature french-made Hectate II 50.cal sniper rifle. Sculptor Serika Misaki has once again brought one of anime's most deadly vixens to life with DD Sinon, and with DOLPA out of the way, DD Sinon is already fetching quite a heafty price on the online market. Although I hope to see more Vocaloid Dollfies in the near future (*crosses fingers for a Luka DD*) DD Sinon was a pleasant surprise indeed.For more information on this story please visit the Official Volks website here.
